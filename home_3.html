Zindgi berang

Unhone rang kya badla apna,
Zindgi kisi ki berang ho gyi,
Barso ki thi wo siddat wali mohobbat,
Jo bas ek chutki sindoor me kisi aur ke sang ho gyi.

By : Abhishek kr Jha

Khamosh rehne ka

Khamosh rehne ka dil nhi kr rha,
Chalo na do pal baat kr lete hai,
Tanhaiyo me jeena hme bhi pasand nhi,
Chalo na isi bahaney mulaakat kr lete hai.

By : Somesh

Kadmo me jahan

Mushkilon se bhaag jaana aasaan hota h,
Har pal zindgi ka imtihaan hota h,
Darne walo ko kaha milta kuchh zindagi me,
Ladne walo ke kadmo me jahan hota h.

By : Rakshit Raj

Ek dusre k liye aansu bahate rhe,
bss intzar ek dusre ko krwate rhe,
wo aakr haledarmaiya hmare dil ka puchhenge,

Arso ke baad

Arso k baad koi dil k itne karib aaya h
Tere aate hi bahut kuchh Maine paya h
Tum rahna sath hmesa kuchh iss tarah
Jese rahta mere sath mera Saya h.

By :Anushree

Har juban pr

Har juban pr ek din tera hi naam hoga,
har kadam pr tere duniya ka salaam hoga,
Har musibat ka samnaa krna tu dat kr,
Tu dekhna samay tera bhi gulam hoga.

By :Anjali
