Har juban pr

Har juban pr ek din tera hi naam hoga,
har kadam pr tere duniya ka salaam hoga,
Har musibat ka samnaa krna tu dat kr,
Tu dekhna samay tera bhi gulam hoga.

By :Anjali

Kya hai Nasha tere pyar mein

Kya hai Nasha tere pyar mein,
Ki dil kre karta rhu izhar mai,
Tere sath kahi kho jau iss kadar,
Ki mil na pau purey sansaar mein.

By : Anmol

Seene se laga kr

Seene se lga kr baitha hu tere tasweer ko,
Tera sath sayd manjoor nhi mere taqdeer ko,
Chahat to puri ki jaati hai bade logo ki aaj kal,
Tavi to khuda bhi nhi sunna chahta iss faqeer ko.

By : Gulshan

***

Jo tu hath tham kr

Jo tu hath tham kr mere sath rahti hai,
Mere har dard me mujhe khusi jhalakti hai,
Tere bina suni suni ho jati hai meri duniya,
Quki dil ke har kone me tu hi to basti hai.

By : Animesh Jha

Har khwahis tujhse judi hai

Har aarzoo, har khwahis tujhse judi hai,
Saanse bhi abb tujhme basi hai,
Chahat ka silsila kuchh iss qadar hai,
Ki teri muskurahat hi abb meri hasi hai.

By : Sonali Raj

Ek dusre k liye

Ek dusre k liye aansu bahate rhe,
bss intzar ek dusre ko krwate rhe,
wo aakr haledarmaiya hmare dil ka puchhenge,
Dilasha bss yhi dil ko dono dilate rhe.

By : Anuska
