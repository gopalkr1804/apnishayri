Khamosh rehne ka

Khamosh rehne ka dil nhi kr rha,
Chalo na do pal baat kr lete hai,
Tanhaiyo me jeena hme bhi pasand nhi,
Chalo na isi bahaney mulaakat kr lete hai.

By : Somesh

Gulab si teri mahak

Gulab si teri mahak saanso me chha jati hai
Chuu lu jism tere to makhmali si ehsaas aa jati hai
mere rago me bass gyi hai tu kuchh iss qadar
saanse chalti teri aur jaan mere andar aa jati hai.

Kya hai Nasha tere pyar mein

Kya hai Nasha tere pyar mein,
Ki dil kre karta rhu izhar mai,
Tere sath kahi kho jau iss kadar,
Ki mil na pau purey sansaar mein.

By : Anmol

Arso ke baad

Arso k baad koi dil k itne karib aaya h
Tere aate hi bahut kuchh Maine paya h
Tum rahna sath hmesa kuchh iss tarah
Jese rahta mere sath mera Saya h.

By :Anushree
