Arso ke baad

Arso k baad koi dil k itne karib aaya h
Tere aate hi bahut kuchh Maine paya h
Tum rahna sath hmesa kuchh iss tarah
Jese rahta mere sath mera Saya h.

By :Anushree

Kadmo me jahan

Mushkilon se bhaag jaana aasaan hota h,
Har pal zindgi ka imtihaan hota h,
Darne walo ko kaha milta kuchh zindagi me,
Ladne walo ke kadmo me jahan hota h.

By : Rakshit Raj

Taqdeer ke khel

Taqdeer ke khel se nirash nhi hote,
Zindgi me aise kvi udas nhi hote,
Hatho ki lakeero pr kyun bharosa krte ho,
Taqdeer unki bhi hoti jinke hath nhi hote.

By : Shakshi Suman

Dhoka ye wafa ki

Dhoka ye wafa ki raah me khaye h hm jrur,
Lekin kisi ke sath me dhoka nhi kiya,
Hmne guzar di hai fakiri me jindgi,
Lekin kvi zameer ka suada nhi kiya.

By : Rahul Sharma
