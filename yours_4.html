Apka pyar mang liya

Tutne aur bikhrne ka chalan mang liya,
Humne haalaat se sise ka badan mang liya,
Hum bhi khade the taqdeer ke darwaze pe,
Log daulat pr girey aur hmne apka pyar mang liya.

By : Mukesh

Tumse mohobbat krne ki

Tumse mohobbat karne ki jo gunah ki hai,
Bepannah ishq krke tumne uski saza di hai,
Upar wale ne hi hmari kismat sath likhi thi,
Humne to bss unke kalam ki karz adaa ki hai.

By : Munmun

Yun toh manzil

Yun toh manzilon ka mohtaaj nahi tha mera pyar,
Dekhna agle janam me firse tumhe takhraenge,
Tumne waqt samajh karr guzar diya tha hame ,
Aur ham zindagi samajh kar aajh bhi jee rahaein hai.

By : SayedSohail

Mohobbat ibadat hai

Mohobbat ibadat hai, aur mai mere yaar se krta hu,
Mujhe to nhi pta mohobbat ka majhab, mai to bss nabi ke faristo se pyar krta hu,
Aur agar hota hai mohobbat me bhi dharm aur majhab,
To mai ise mohobbat kehne se inqaar krta hu.

By : Abhishek kr Jha

Dard jo dil me

Dard jo dil mai hai, mai wo byan kr nhi sakta,

Kahaani puri kr na saka apni to kya ho gya,
Km se km do char panne to hai paltaane ko.
