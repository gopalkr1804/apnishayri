Seene se laga kr

Seene se lga kr baitha hu tere tasweer ko,
Tera sath sayd manjoor nhi mere taqdeer ko,
Chahat to puri ki jaati hai bade logo ki aaj kal,
Tavi to khuda bhi nhi sunna chahta iss faqeer ko.

By : Gulshan
