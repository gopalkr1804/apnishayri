Mohobbat nhi

Mohobbat nhi mukkaddr me hamari,
Yhi sochkr chle jaa rhe the
Mohobbat nhi mukkaddr me hamari,
Yhi sochkr chle jaa rhe the
Khuda ki karamat dekho, mudkar dekha
to tum bhi akele hi aa rhe the.

By : Shreya

Uske ghar ka rashta

Mujhe yaad v nhi rhta ab khud ka naam mera,
Pr wo saksh mujhe yaad hai,
Khuli aankho se bhi bhatak jata hu apne ghar ka pata,
Aur band ankhon se bhi uske ghar ka rashta yaad hai.

By : Abhishek kr Jha

Rashta hi badl liya

Wo mud gye hume dekh kr, rashta hi badal liya
Kbhi isi surat ko dekhne ki talab thi, aaj wo waqt badl gya,
Mana jaane anjane galtiyan mene ki, pr ek do gushtakhi to aapse v huyi,
Bas samjh nhi aa rha ki saja apne di, humne di ya kismat ne hume diya.

By : Simran

Toot gye sare sapne

Tut gye sare sapne, bikhar gye sare jazbaat,
Yakeen mano mere dost,
Na hi koi gum hai mujhe, aur na hi kuchh yaad,
Qki fr kuchh bura hua hi nhi meri jindgi me ek tere jane ke baad.

By : Abhishek kr Jha

Zindgi berang

Unhone rang kya badla apna,
Zindgi kisi ki berang ho gyi,
Barso ki thi wo siddat wali mohobbat,
Jo bas ek chutki sindoor me kisi aur ke sang ho gyi.

By : Abhishek kr Jha

Khamosh rehne ka

Khamosh rehne ka dil nhi kr rha,
Chalo na do pal baat kr lete hai,
Tanhaiyo me jeena hme bhi pasand nhi,
Chalo na isi bahaney mulaakat kr lete hai.

By : Somesh
