Yun toh manzil

Yun toh manzilon ka mohtaaj nahi tha mera pyar,
Dekhna agle janam me firse tumhe takhraenge,
Tumne waqt samajh karr guzar diya tha hame ,
Aur ham zindagi samajh kar aajh bhi jee rahaein hai.

By : SayedSohail

Jindgi ke iss safar me

Jindgi ke iss safar me ek mod pr tu mil gyi

dost bani, sath chali, dosti se v aagey nikal gyi

Gulab si teri mahak

Gulab si teri mahak saanso me chha jati hai
Chuu lu jism tere to makhmali si ehsaas aa jati hai
mere rago me bass gyi hai tu kuchh iss qadar
saanse chalti teri aur jaan mere andar aa jati hai.

Din dhalta nhi
