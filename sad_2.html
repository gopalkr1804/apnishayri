Zindgi berang

Unhone rang kya badla apna,
Zindgi kisi ki berang ho gyi,
Barso ki thi wo siddat wali mohobbat,
Jo bas ek chutki sindoor me kisi aur ke sang ho gyi.

By : Abhishek kr Jha

Tu dur rahna chahe

Tu dur rhna chahe to isme bura kya h,
Tujhe pyar na huwa to huwa kya h,
Jaruri to nhi hr chahne wale ko uski chahat mil hii jaye,

Dhoka ye wafa ki

Dhoka ye wafa ki raah me khaye h hm jrur,
Lekin kisi ke sath me dhoka nhi kiya,
Hmne guzar di hai fakiri me jindgi,
Lekin kvi zameer ka suada nhi kiya.

By : Rahul Sharma
