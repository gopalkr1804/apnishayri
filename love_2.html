Jindgi ke iss safar me

Jindgi ke iss safar me ek mod pr tu mil gyi
Andhere se bhari iss jindgi me, dhup si tu khil gyi
dost bani, sath chali, dosti se v aagey nikal gyi
Wo waqt tha hum anjaan the, ab to jaan ban gyi.

***

Apki aankhon se

Apki aankhon se ishq jhalkta hai,
Pr apka dil byan krne se darta hai,
Sarmaana to apki aadat si ban gyi hai,
Par apki sarmahat me bhi pyar jhalkta hai.

***

Tumse mohobbat krne ki

Tumse mohobbat karne ki jo gunah ki hai,
Bepannah ishq krke tumne uski saza di hai,
Upar wale ne hi hmari kismat sath likhi thi,
Humne to bss unke kalam ki karz adaa ki hai.

By : Munmun

***

Jo tu hath tham kr

Jo tu hath tham kr mere sath rahti hai,
Mere har dard me mujhe khusi jhalakti hai,
Tere bina suni suni ho jati hai meri duniya,
Quki dil ke har kone me tu hi to basti hai.

By : Animesh Jha

***

Har khwahis tujhse judi hai

Har aarzoo, har khwahis tujhse judi hai,
Saanse bhi abb tujhme basi hai,
Chahat ka silsila kuchh iss qadar hai,
Ki teri muskurahat hi abb meri hasi hai.

By : Sonali Raj

***

Jab mere Hatho

Jab mere Hatho me tera hath hota h,
Jab tanhai me tera sath hota h,
Byan kr na Pata mai apni khusi ko,
Pr sayd tujhe v wo ehsas hota h.
